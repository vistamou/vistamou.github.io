About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Natural Language Processing at Archimedes AI (Athena Research Center), working at the intersection of computational linguistics and language resources. My work focuses on building and curating linguistic datasets, advancing tools for under-resourced languages, and studying bias and harmful language in AI systems.

Research Focus

Low-resource languages Treebank creation, morphological annotation, and UD resources.
Lexicons for safety Offensive language detection and multilingual lexicon development.
Multimodality and AI bias Human-centered analysis of AI systems and media installations.
